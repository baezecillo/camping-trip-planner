# Reflection

This assignment was more challenging than I expected. The entire concept
of loop engineering was new to me until the class we had. I spent some
time trying to understand it's concept, and proceeded to use Claude to
structure a plan since I wanted to go on a step-by-step process to fully
grasp this concept as much as I could.

Now that I have completed the process, it's very clear that experience,
documentation and clarity of the goal in mind are more important than
ever. I am very experienced with backend, but frontend is something that
I stepped aside from, but with the aid of AI in combination of many
prompts I managed to define, develop, and locally test an idea that would
have taken way longer for me to implement.

I even went further than "making it work" as with the base system was
complete, I had now the time to question "how to make specifications for
look and feel" and even wonder how to step away from happy paths and start
making the current implementation safer and faster.

I think loop engineering is a powerful concept, and no matter if you are in
favor or against AI, the reality is that these kind of tools are the reality
in 2026, and we should learn how to leverage them.
